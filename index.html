This policy explains how we handle your data, our subscription model, advertising practices, and details all permissions used by the app.

1. App Overview

  • Age Requirement: TaskMate is designed for users aged 13 and above
  • Free with Premium: The app offers a free tier with basic features and a premium subscription for enhanced functionality
  • Offline-First: Core functionality works without internet connection
  • Privacy-Focused: Built with privacy as a fundamental design principle

2. Data Storage and Ownership

  • Local First: All your tasks, events, and personal data are stored locally on your device by default
  • Complete Control: You have full control over your data, with options to export, backup, and delete at any time
  • Your Data Belongs to You: We design our app with the philosophy that your data is yours alone. We don't claim ownership over any content you create
  • Premium Cloud Storage: Premium subscribers can optionally enable secure cloud sync for data backup and multi-device access

3. Data Collection and Usage

  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only the data you explicitly create within the app and necessary technical information
  • Types of Data:
    • Tasks and their details
    • Events and calendar entries
    • Categories and task classifications
    • App preferences and settings
    • Subscription status and billing information (processed by Google Play/App Store)
    • Device information for advertising (free tier only)
  • Usage Analytics: Basic, anonymous usage statistics may be collected to improve the app experience. This can be disabled in settings
  • Advertising Data: For free users, we may collect device advertising identifiers and usage patterns to show relevant ads

4. App Permissions

TaskMate requires certain permissions to deliver its full functionality. All permissions are requested only when needed and can be managed in your device settings at any time.

Permission Purpose Optional?
INTERNET Required for cloud sync (Premium), ads (Free), and receiving remote notifications Required
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check network connectivity for cloud sync and ad loading Required
VIBRATE Used for haptic feedback with notifications and reminders Optional
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Allows the app to restart scheduled notifications after device reboot Optional
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Enables task reminders and notifications Optional
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Required for precise task deadline notifications Optional
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Ensures notifications work reliably even with battery optimization Optional
READ_CALENDAR Allows importing events from your device calendar (read-only) Optional
WRITE_CALENDAR Allows creating task-related events in your device calendar Optional
AD_ID Used for personalized advertising in the free version Optional (Free users)
Important: Most permissions are optional and can be disabled at any time. Core app functionality works without these permissions, though certain features may be limited. Premium users can disable advertising-related permissions.

5. Subscriptions and Premium Features

TaskMate offers two tiers:

  • Free Tier: Basic task management with ads
  • Premium Subscription: Ad-free experience with advanced features
  • Billing: All subscription billing is handled securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • Data Collection: We only receive subscription status information (active/inactive) from the app stores
  • Payment Information: We never store or have access to your credit card or payment details
  • Premium Benefits:
    • Ad-free experience
    • Cloud sync across devices
    • Advanced task features
    • Priority customer support
    • Enhanced privacy controls
  • Cancellation: You can cancel your subscription at any time through your app store account

6. Cloud Sync Premium Only

Cloud sync is available exclusively for Premium subscribers!

This feature allows you to:

  • Securely sync your tasks across multiple devices
  • Back up your data to the cloud
  • Access your tasks from anywhere
  • Seamlessly switch between devices
  • Opt-in Only: Cloud sync is completely optional and requires explicit activation by Premium users
  • Authentication: We use secure authentication methods to protect your data
  • Encryption: All synced data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • User Control: You have complete control over what data is synced and when
  • Data Location: Cloud data is stored on secure servers with enterprise-grade security
  • Deletion: You can delete all cloud data at any time from the app settings
  • No Ads: Premium users' cloud data is never used for advertising purposes
Privacy First: Even with cloud sync enabled, we maintain our privacy-first approach. Your task data remains private and is never shared with third parties.

7. Advertising (Free Users Only)

To support the free version of TaskMate, we display advertisements to non-premium users:

  • Ad Networks: We partner with reputable advertising networks (Google AdMob) to display ads
  • Data for Ads:
    • Device advertising identifier (can be reset in device settings)
    • Basic device information (OS version, device type)
    • App usage patterns (anonymized)
    • General location (country/region level only)
  • Personalized Ads: Ads may be personalized based on your interests and usage patterns
  • Ad Preferences: You can control ad personalization through your device's advertising settings
  • Premium Users: Premium subscribers enjoy a completely ad-free experience with no advertising data collection
  • Opt-out: You can limit ad tracking through your device settings or upgrade to Premium
Important: Task content, personal information, and sensitive data are never shared with advertising networks. Only anonymized usage patterns and device identifiers are used for ad targeting.

8. Third-Party Integrations

  • System Calendar: Read-only access to your device's calendar (optional)
  • Firebase Services: We use Firebase for cloud messaging, notifications, and Premium cloud sync
  • Google Play Services: For subscription management and some device features
  • Advertising Networks: Google AdMob for free users (can be disabled with Premium subscription)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance (can be opted out)
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell your personal data to any third parties
  • Limited Sharing: We don't share your information with third parties except when explicitly required to provide requested functionality

9. Data Security

  • Local Storage: Shared Preferences and encrypted local databases for secure, persistent data storage
  • Cloud Security: Enterprise-grade encryption for Premium cloud sync
  • Best Practices: We follow industry best practices for data security and privacy
  • Offline First: No internet connection required for core functionality
  • Data Encryption: Application data is stored with encryption on your device
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures and protocols

10. User Controls

  • Permission Management: All permissions can be managed through your device settings
  • In-App Settings: Detailed controls for notifications, reminders, background services, and privacy preferences
  • Data Export: Export and import data at any time in standard formats
  • Local Data Deletion: Delete all local data with a single tap
  • Complete Data Deletion: Request deletion of all your data (including cloud data) through our Data Deletion Request Portal
  • In-App Deletion: Direct data deletion functionality coming in the next app update
  • Cloud Controls: Premium users can enable/disable cloud sync and delete cloud data independently
  • Ad Controls: Free users can manage ad preferences through device settings
  • Analytics Opt-out: Disable usage analytics in app settings
  • Granular Control: Choose what data to backup, share, or delete
  • Subscription Management: Manage your Premium subscription through your app store account

Data Deletion Options:

  • Local Data: Delete app data directly from your device through app settings
  • Complete Deletion: Request deletion of all data (including cloud data) through our Data Deletion Request Portal
  • Coming Soon: Direct in-app deletion of all data will be available in the next update
Privacy First: TaskMate is built with a privacy-first approach. All features that could potentially impact your privacy are disabled by default and require your explicit opt-in.

11. Age Requirements

  • Minimum Age: TaskMate is intended for users aged 13 and above
  • Parental Consent: Users between 13-17 should have parental consent before using the app
  • Data Protection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Reporting: If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information about a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information
Important: If you are under 13, please do not use TaskMate or provide any personal information through the app.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

  • Updates: We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, features, or for legal/regulatory reasons
  • Notification: Significant changes will be notified to you through the app and via email if you have cloud sync enabled
  • Latest Version: The current version will always be available within the app
  • Continued Use: Your continued use of the app after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Major Changes: For significant changes affecting your privacy rights, we may require explicit consent
