1. Data Storage and Ownership

  • Local First: All your tasks, events, and personal data are stored locally on your device using SQLite database.
  • Complete Control: You have full control over your data, with options to export, backup, and delete at any time.
  • Your Data Belongs to You: We design our app with the philosophy that your data is yours alone. We don't claim ownership over any content you create.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only the data you explicitly create within the app.
  • Types of Data:
    • Tasks and their details
    • Events and calendar entries
    • Categories and task classifications
    • App preferences and settings
  • Usage Analytics: Basic, anonymous usage statistics may be collected to improve the app experience. This can be disabled in settings.

3. Optional Features and Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send task and event reminders
  • Calendar Sync: Optional system calendar event import (read-only)
  • Storage: For backup and restore functionality
Important: All permissions are optional and can be disabled at any time. No permissions are required for core app functionality.

4. Cloud Sync Coming Soon

We're excited to announce that cloud sync is coming soon to TaskMate!

When this feature becomes available, you'll be able to:

  • Securely sync your tasks across multiple devices
  • Back up your data to the cloud
  • Access your tasks from anywhere

All cloud features will be completely optional and will follow our strict privacy-first approach.

  • Authentication: We'll use secure authentication methods to protect your data
  • Encryption: All synced data will be encrypted in transit and at rest
  • User Control: You'll have complete control over what data is synced and when
  • Transparency: We'll provide clear information about data storage and handling

5. Third-Party Integrations

  • System Calendar: Read-only access to your device's calendar (optional)
  • Future Integrations: Any future third-party integrations will be clearly documented and optional
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell your data to any third parties
  • Limited Sharing: We don't share your information with third parties except when explicitly required to provide requested functionality

6. Data Security

  • Local Storage: Local SQLite database with secure storage
  • Best Practices: We follow industry best practices for data security
  • Offline First: No internet connection required for core functionality
  • Data Encryption: Application data is stored with encryption on your device

7. User Controls

  • Data Export: Export and import data at any time in standard formats
  • Data Deletion: Delete all data with a single tap
  • Preferences: Customize your privacy settings within the app
  • Granular Control: Choose what data to backup, share, or delete
Privacy First: TaskMate is built with a privacy-first approach. All features that could potentially impact your privacy are disabled by default and require your explicit opt-in.

8. Children's Privacy

TaskMate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information about a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The latest version will always be available within the app, and significant changes will be notified to you.
